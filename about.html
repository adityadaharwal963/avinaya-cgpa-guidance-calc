About Project

• Team Avinaya's project helps to guide college students to achieve their desired CGPA through different aspects of the system and is simple as follows :
• Our "CGPA guidance calculator" web application will take a student's marks as input and the target CGPA as input from the user.
• Our algorithm will compute the value of CGPA and recommend the user to do various courses to gain incentives.
• It will also give target marks for the next semester according to the target CGPA.


Conclusion

Therefore team Avinaya's project will be beneficial and supportive for the students.
